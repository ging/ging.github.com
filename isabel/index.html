This anniversary commemorates the first event performed with the Isabel application, namely the second Summer School on Advanced Broadband Communication in July 1994, a 3 day event connecting 5 auditoriums located in Spain, Portugal and Switzerland. Speakers and attendees could attend in any of the sites. ISABEL connected over the first ATM broadband infrastructures to demonstrate the power of broadband applications.
